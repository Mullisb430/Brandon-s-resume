Beginnings

Born and raised in a small rural town.

Throughout Brandon's childhood, his family moved around quite a bit in central Georgia. He was born in a small town called Lizella, and that's where he spent the majority of his early childhood. His family then moved to Monroe county, then to Bibb county, and then moved to a couple of places around Bibb county. He now resides in a small town north of Macon Georgia, called Forsyth.

Brandon spends most of his days working for an Ambulance transportation company in North Macon. He spends most of his nights doing school work, and learning how to code. He enjoys exercising, and hanging out with friends and family. Brandon is a very orderly person and prides himself on organization. He keeps a very tight schedule, and likes to use his time as efficiently as possible.
